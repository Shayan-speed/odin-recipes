Fried chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces (thighs or breasts): 6
  • Milk or yogurt: 1 cup
  • Flour: 1 cup
  • Eggs: 2
  • Salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder: to taste
  • Cooking oil: enough for frying

Cooking steps

  1. Marinate the chicken: Mix chicken with milk or yogurt, salt, pepper, and spices. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Prepare coating: Beat eggs in a bowl, and place flour in another
  3. Coat the chicken: Dip each piece of chicken first in the eggs, then in flour until fully coated.
  4. Fry the chicken: Heat oil in a skillet and fry the chicken over medium heat until golden and fully cooked (about 15â€“20 minutes depending on piece size).
  5. Serve: Place fried chicken on paper towels to remove excess oil and serve hot.
