Ghormeh Sabzi

Ingredients

  • Stew beef: 300 g
  • Kidney beans or pinto beans: ½ cup
  • Ghormeh Sabzi herbs (leek/chives, parsley, cilantro, fenugreek): 300 g
  • Onion: 1 large
  • Dried limes (limoo amani): 2–3
  • Salt, black pepper, turmeric: to taste
  • Oil: 3–4 tbsp
  • Water: as needed

Cooking steps

  1. Sauté the onion: Finely chop the onion and sauté in oil with turmeric until golden..
  2. Add the meat: Add the beef and sauté until it changes color.
  3. Add the beans: Add the soaked beans to the pot.
  4. Fry the herbs: In a separate pan, fry the herbs well in a little oil until they become darker.
  5. Combine herbs: Add the fried herbs to the pot.
  6. Add water and dried limes: Pour in water, poke holes in the dried limes, and drop them into the stew.
  7. Add water and dried limes: Pour in water, poke holes in the dried limes, and drop them into the stew.
  8. Slow cooking: Cover and let the stew simmer for 2–3 hours until fully cooked and flavorful.
  9. Add salt: Add salt in the last 30 minutes to prevent the beans from hardening.
