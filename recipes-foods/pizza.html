Pizza

Ingredients

  • Flour: 2 cups
  • Warm water: 1 cup
  • Yeast: 1 tablespoon
  • Yeast: 1 tablespoon
  • Sugar: 1 teaspoon
  • Salt: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Oil: 2 tablespoons
  • Mozzarella cheese: as needed
  • Tomato sauce or pizza sauce: a few tablespoons

Cooking steps

  1. Activate the yeast: Mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 10 minutes.
  2. Make the dough: Add flour, salt, and oil. Knead until the dough becomes smooth.
  3. Let it rise: Cover the dough and rest it for about 1 hour until it doubles in size.
  4. Prepare the base: Roll out the dough and place it on a baking tray. Spread some pizza sauce on it.
  5. Add cheese: Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.
  6. Bake: Bake at 180°C (356°F) for 20–25 minutes until the cheese becomes golden.
